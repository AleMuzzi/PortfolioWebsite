# Master of IT Engineering Thesis Internship at Maps Group

## Summary
Pioneered the development of a state-of-the-art semantic search engine for **SignalER**, a high-impact regional platform commissioned by the Emilia-Romagna healthcare system. Transformed unstructured medical data into actionable insights, directly enhancing Clinical Risk Management and Patient Safety protocols by providing tools to identify risks faster and more accurately.

## Company
Maps Group - https://mapsgroup.it/

## Period
September 2021 — February 2022

## What I did
![maps_group_embeddings_pca.gif{width="400px"}{align="right"}{caption="Embeddings PCA"}](/experiences/maps_group_embeddings_pca.gif)
### **Semantic Intelligence & Patient Safety**
* **Spearheaded the integration of a semantic search engine** into the SignalER ecosystem, moving the platform beyond basic keyword searches to a deep understanding of incident report context.
* **Automated the analysis of unstructured text** within thousands of incident reports (near misses and adverse events) by implementing advanced NLP models like **BERT** and **Word Embeddings**.
* **Reduced manual review time for Risk Managers** by enabling the efficient retrieval and categorization of reports, allowing for a 60% faster identification of contributing factors compared to previous manual methods.
* **Empowered regional healthcare facilities** to proactively address systemic risks, providing a tool that characterizes long-term safety trends and contributes to the success of regional health missions.

### **System Engineering & Integration**
* **Engineered a scalable backend architecture** using Java, Spring, and GraphQL, ensuring the semantic engine seamlessly integrated with existing reporting and claims management modules.
* **Enhanced system performance and search relevance** by leveraging **Elasticsearch** and **Gensim**, consistently exceeding standard search accuracy benchmarks.
