# Internship for thesis in Master of IT Engineering at Maps Group

## Summary
Study and development of a semantic search engine for **SignalER**, an incident reporting platform commissioned by the Emilia-Romagna region.

## Company
Maps Group - https://mapsgroup.it/

## Period
September 2021 — February 2022

## What I did
Semantic search engine for the **SignalER** platform:

* Contributed to the development of **SignalER**, a regional platform managed by **Maps Group** designed to digitize **Clinical Risk Management** and enhance **Patient Safety** across healthcare facilities.
* Designed and implemented a **semantic search engine** to analyze unstructured text within incident reports, such as descriptions of "near misses" and adverse events.
* Enabled Risk Managers to efficiently retrieve and categorize reports, identifying contributing factors to incidents using Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques.
* The system was integrated into the broader **SignalER** ecosystem, which manages workflows for reporting, validation, and claims management for the Emilia-Romagna healthcare system.

## Technologies and tools

- **Languages:** Python, Java
- **Frameworks:** Spring Framework
- **Data & AI:** Machine Learning, BERT, Gensim, Word Embeddings
- **Infrastructure:** GraphQL, Elasticsearch, Docker
