# Tirocinio per tesi magistrale at Maps Group

## Summary
Development of a semantic search engine for health care incident reports.

## Period
September 2021 — February 2022

## What i did
- Study and development of a semantic search engine to search for incident reports in the health care sector.
- The search engine is part of a larger project for the development of a smart management platform for incident reports commissioned by the Emilia Romagna region.

## Technologies and tools
- **Elasticsearch**
- **BERT** (Language Model)
- **Python**
- **Spring Framework**
- **Java**
- **Word Embeddings**
- **GraphQL**
